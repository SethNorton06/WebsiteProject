Mission Statement

Our goal is to find loving and caring homes for animals that are in need for a good family and educate pet owners and prospective pet owners in the Stevens County area.


Contact Information

You can reach our email at StevensCountyAnimalShelter@gmail.com and can call us anytime between 9am-5pm Monday-Friday and feel free to stop by the shelter within the same hours at 13640 W Solano Dr Litchfield Park, Arizona.


Donations

We do not have a way to donate online, but you can donate in person at the shelter at 13640 W Solano Dr Litchfield Park, Arizona.
